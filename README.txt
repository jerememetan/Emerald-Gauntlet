This is a ROM Hack of Pokemon Emerald, created with decomp hacking from Pokemerald Expansion. It is designed to be Nuzlocked, focusing on survival of gauntlets of fights with limited resources.

FEATURES:
Gauntlets, a lot of them
Pokemon and moves up to gen-9
Mega Evolutions
Level Caps
Gen 9 Snow mechanics and Frostbite
Boss Battles


HOW TO PLAY
Download a GBA Emulator
Load the game into GBA Emulator
